Chaufa Rice

Description

This is my favourite dish (and the one I cook the best). It is also a product from the fusion of peruvian and chinese culture. It is really liked among peruvians. It is commonly accompanied with Inca Kola, which is a peruvian soda.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of rice
  • 2 scallions
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 piece of ginger
  • 1 chicken breast (chopped in squares)
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of oyster sauce

Preparation

  1. Cook the rice without any seasoning.
  2. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  3. Chop the bulb of the scallion and the ginger in small dices. This is called Cuchikay.
  4. Make an omelette with the eggs and chop it into dices.
  5. Heat a large pan and put some vegetable oil.
  6. Add cuchikay and cook for 30 seconds. Then add the chicken and cook for about 5-7 minutes. Reserve.
  7. In the same pan, add the rice and soy sauce. Stir until the color is evenly distributed.
  8. Add the diced egg and the chicken. Stir and then add the oyster sauce and sesame oil.
  9. Chopp the scallions and add it on top. Stir and serve.

This recipe can seem complex at first, but it is actually really simple. Enjoy!
