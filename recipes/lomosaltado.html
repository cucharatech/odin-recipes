Lomo Saltado

Description

This is one of my favourite peruvian dishes. It is a fusion between chinese and peruvian cuisine. The smell and taste are quite extraordinary.

This recipe it's easy and quick. You can have it done in less than 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 500gr of steak, cut into strips
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced without the seeds
  • 4 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • Oyster Sauce, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon parsley

Preparation

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper and let it rest for about 10 minutes. At the same time, heat the pan. It must be in "smoke point".
  2. Once the pan is in "smoke point", add the steak with some oil and cook until browned for about 5 minutes. Don't let it parboil. Remove from the pan and reserve.
  3. In the same pan, add some oil and cook the onions for about 2 minutes and then add the tomatos. Add 2 more minutes.
  4. Add the steak, soy sauce, vinegar, oyster sauce and parsley in that order. Stir to combine and let it cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Serve with a side of fries and rice if desired.
  6. Enjoy!

I wish you enjoy it as much as I do.
